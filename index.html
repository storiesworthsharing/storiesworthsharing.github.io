About Us

We are a content company started by two engineering graduates as sophomores in college. We conduct storytelling sessions in different cities of India with an intent to revive the human interaction. A network with mass presence and more than 100 sessions, we are just getting started!

Need more reasons to join us here? In an effort to carry the love home as a souvenir, every attendee of our session takes back a handwritten postcard. These postcards are inscribed with a personalized quote curated for the respective community member. Is not it cool enough in this digital age? Know more

Our Vision

We envision our venture to benefit Indian youth and create the largest storytelling platform for tier-II, tier-III, and tier IV Indian cities.
